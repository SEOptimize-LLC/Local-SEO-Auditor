## PART 2: CRITICAL 2026 RANKING SIGNALS (CORRECTED)

### 1. BUSINESS HOURS "OPENNESS" SIGNAL - NOW A CRITICAL FILTER

**What Changed**: "Openness" evolved from minor signal to **critical filter** that removes businesses from results entirely.

#### How Openness Works:
- **Immediate-need queries** ("emergency plumber," "pizza near me," "AC repair now") → Google FILTERS OUT completely any business that is closed at search time
- **Non-immediate queries** ("best plumber Denver") → Hours have ranking impact but business still appears
- **Real-time mechanism**: Google checks business hours against current time/timezone in real-time

#### Competitive Advantage Strategy:
If your 3 competitors all close at 9pm but you stay open until 11pm, you capture 100% of emergency calls between 9-11pm—they're completely filtered out.

#### Audit Action:
1. Check GBP hours are 100% accurate (test: what does Google show in Maps right now?)
2. Compare hours to top 3 competitors (identify time windows you're the only option)
3. Consider extended hours during peak seasons (winter for plumbing, summer for HVAC)
4. If offering emergency service: list "24/7 Available" or "Emergency Hours"

#### Impact Data:
- Businesses with accurate hours: +15% Local Map clicks during off-hours
- Businesses staying open when competitors close: +40% call volume during those windows

### 2. REVIEW SIGNALS - NOW 20% OF LOCAL MAP PACK (DOUBLED IMPORTANCE)

**What Changed**: My original estimate of ~10% was incorrect. Reviews now account for **20%** of Local Map Pack ranking signals.

This makes reviews your **PRIMARY ranking lever** (tied with GBP signals at 32% combined focus).

#### The Review Components (All Matter):

**A) Review Volume**
- 10-review threshold: Significant jump in ranking position
- Target baseline: 20+ reviews (local businesses averaging 15-30)
- Benchmark gap: If competitors have 100+ and you have 45, you have a 55-review deficit

**B) Review Rating**
- 4.7+ rating is minimum competitive threshold
- 4.8+ is strong position
- Gap of -0.1 vs competitor matters more with high volume

**C) Review Velocity (NOW MORE IMPORTANT THAN VOLUME)**
- **2 consistent reviews/month** > 20 reviews once yearly
- Algorithm favors ongoing engagement signal
- Momentum matters: growing review count signals active business

**D) Review Keywords & Content**
- Reviews mentioning your service name rank you for more variations
- Example: Reviews saying "emergency drain cleaning" help you rank for that phrase
- Negative keywords in reviews hurt ranking (watch for complaint patterns)

**E) Review Diversity**
- Reviews from different customers across geographic service area
- If all reviews from one neighborhood = signals limited service area

**F) Reviewer Authority & Recency**
- Recent reviews weighted more heavily
- Verified local reviewer status carries more weight
- Old reviews (6+ months) weighted less

#### 90-Day Review Generation Target (UPDATED):
- **Aggressive target**: 20-30 new reviews (1/3rd increase)
- **Conservative target**: 15-20 new reviews
- **Monthly velocity**: 5-10 reviews/month ongoing

#### Multi-Channel Review Collection:
